ChaosPlayground is an interactive, sandbox-style simulation tool for software and systems engineers to safely practice DevOps, Site Reliability Engineering (SRE), and operations workflows.

By intentionally injecting controlled infrastructure failures, performance bottlenecks, and service outages, the platform creates a realistic war room environment — without touching production.

The aim

Bridge the gap between theoretical system design and hands-on crisis management. Build troubleshooting muscle memory, interpret chaotic metrics, and practice incident response in a safe, local environment.
